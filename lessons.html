Introduction to Touch Typing

Touch Typing is the skill of typing without looking at the keyboard. It helps you type faster, more accurately, and with less strain.

Sitting Position

  • Sit upright with your back straight.
  • Keep feet flat on the floor.
  • Wrists should be slightly raised, not resting on the keyboard.
  • Maintain a comfortable distance from the screen.

Hand Positioning

Your fingers rest on the Home Row Keys: A S D F (left hand) and J K L ; (right hand). Thumbs rest on the Spacebar.

Typing Metrics

WPM (Words Per Minute): Measures typing speed. One word = 5 characters.

Accuracy: Percentage of correctly typed characters/words.

AWPM (Adjusted WPM): WPM after deducting errors (true typing speed).
